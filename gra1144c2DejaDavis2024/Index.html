WELCOME

Bailey Hall is an arts center on the main campus of Broward College that provides outreach to the local community, as well as classes in dance, theater, and music. It offers distinctive programming that celebrates the cultures of South Florida and acts as a leader in Broward County's arts education system. Visitors can experience the grandeur and history of the music hall, and student-led events enhance the curriculum. Here's the calendar for upcoming shows and events.
